📖 User Guide

Everything you need to know to play Hacker Simulator 2077.

What is Hacker Simulator 2077?

A terminal-based hacking game where you play as an ethical hacker. Infiltrate servers, evade firewalls, crack passwords, and become the best hacker in the digital underworld.

First Time Setup

  1. Select your language (1=Spanish, 2=English)
  2. Enter your hacker name (or press Enter for "Zero_Cool")
  3. Your configuration is saved in config.json

How to Hack a Server

  1. Choose a server from the list (difficulty 1-20 stars)
  2. Evade the Firewall: Memorize and repeat a sequence of numbers
  3. Crack the Password: Read the clue carefully and type the password
  4. Extra Security: If difficulty >= 5, complete an additional mini-game

Tips for Beginners

  • Read the clues - They tell you exactly what to look for
  • Start with easy servers - Difficulty 1-3 to build your credits
  • Buy the Advanced Scanner first - More attempts = easier passwords
  • Keep your streak - Bonuses add up quickly
  • Do daily missions - Double rewards every day

FAQ

How do I save my game?

Press 8 in the main menu. Your game is saved to save.json.

How do I change the language?

Press 7 (Settings) → 2 (Change language) → Select your language.

How do I delete my account?

Press 7 (Settings) → 3 (Delete account) → Type DELETE to confirm.

Why can't I hack certain servers?

You can only hack servers up to your level + 1. Level up to unlock harder servers.
